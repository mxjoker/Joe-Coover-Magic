Corporate Stage Show

A full theater-style magic show designed specifically for corporate audiences. Joe performs a complete arc of illusions, comedy, and audience interaction — giving your whole room a shared experience they'll be talking about long after the event ends.

This is the flagship show. It works for seated banquets, post-dinner entertainment, conference breaks, and awards ceremonies. Joe handles all logistics including setup and breakdown — your venue team won't even know he was there.

For Cocktail Hours · Trade Shows · Networking

Strolling Close-Up

Joe moves through your event like a guest — then suddenly produces the impossible inches from someone's face. Close-up magic is the most intimate form of performance, and the reactions are unlike anything a stage show can produce.

Perfect for the cocktail hour before dinner, trade show booths that need to draw a crowd, networking mixers, and hospitality suites. Joe works the room in sets, covering every corner of your event.

Audience Size Any size
Duration 1–3 hours
Best For Cocktail hours, Trade shows

Comedy Magic Show

Magic and comedy are better together — and Joe's comedy show leans all the way in. This is a high-energy, audience-driven performance where volunteers become the stars, and laughter is the whole point.

This format shines at holiday parties, team events, and groups that want to laugh together. Joe's crowd work is quick, clever, and completely clean — ideal for mixed corporate crowds at any age.

Mentalism

No props. No gimmicks. Just Joe, your guests, and the apparently impossible. Mentalism is mind-reading, prediction, and psychological mystery performed in an intimate setting — and the reactions tend to be the quietest and the most lasting.

This show works beautifully for executive dinners, VIP receptions, and upscale private events where the vibe calls for sophisticated entertainment over spectacle.
