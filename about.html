The Origin Story

From 2nd Grade Talent Show
to Hollywood — and Back to the Stage

Joe Coover has been fooling people since 2nd grade — and he's gotten considerably better at it. What started as a talent show act followed him all the way to the University of Oklahoma, where he studied on an acting scholarship and earned a filmmaking degree.

He went to Hollywood. He worked in film. And then a few stand-up comedy nights happened — and something clicked. The stage pulled harder than the screen ever did. Joe never looked back.

That pivot turned into a 20+ year career performing for corporations, private events, and audiences across Barcelona, London, Madrid, Istanbul, Vancouver, Las Vegas, New York, Frankfurt, and beyond — including performances on Royal Caribbean and Carnival Cruise Lines. Along the way he's performed for some of the world's most recognizable companies: Facebook, Google, Microsoft, and Lego, among many others.

Joe doesn't do magic at your guests.
He makes them part of it. By the end of the night, your colleagues aren't talking about the tricks — they're talking about the moment Cindy read her boss's mind.
