Origin Story

It Started in 2nd Grade

Joe Coover performed his first magic show at his elementary school talent show. He was eight years old, terrified, and completely hooked. Twenty-plus years later, he's performed for thousands of people across the country — from trade show floors to black-tie galas — and the feeling is still the same.

Along the way, Joe trained seriously. He studied under some of the best close-up performers in the country and competed in the International Brotherhood of Magicians — winning three national titles including the coveted Close-Up Grand Champion award. His style combines technical mastery with genuine humor and audience connection that's hard to fake and impossible to manufacture.

Today Joe specializes in corporate entertainment — because he knows that a room full of skeptical professionals who've seen it all is the best possible audience. When he wins them over (and he always does), it's real.
