Meet Joe

Three Championships.
Twenty Years.
One Show Your Guests Won't Stop Talking About.

Joe started performing in 2nd grade and never really stopped — just kept raising the stakes. Today he builds custom corporate shows that do something most entertainment can't: get a room full of skeptical professionals to lean in, laugh out loud, and walk away genuinely amazed.

Before this was his career, he was a movie projectionist, trapeze instructor, riverboat captain, second-grade teacher, and tiny house builder. A performer shaped by that many rooms doesn't get rattled. He reads crowds for a living — and it shows.
