Meet Joe

Started in 2nd Grade.
Still Blowing Minds.

Joe's first show was his school's talent show — and he's never looked back. After 20+ years and three national championships, he's built a corporate show that combines genuine wonder, sharp humor, and the kind of audience interaction that gets talked about Monday morning.

Along the way he's also been a movie projectionist, trapeze instructor, second-grade teacher, riverboat captain, and tiny house builder. Turns out, a magician with range makes for a better show.
